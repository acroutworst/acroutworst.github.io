FLY - Find Your Sneaker's Worth

Delightful iOS app made in Swift using Xcode, Vision API, and AVFoundation.

Built CoreML model using CustomVision to classify images of varying sneaker types. The goal is to train the majority of popular sneakers and determine a sneaker's worth just by quickly scanning. Anticipating newly-released sneaker drops is also an upcoming, desired feature.

App is in store review and waiting to post the app store link.

Key Insights: AI is fun and useful! Building CoreML models and leveraging real-time object detection.
