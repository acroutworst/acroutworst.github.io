FLY - Find Your Sneaker's Worth

An iOS app made in Swift using Xcode, Vision API, and AVKit (for AVCaptureSession and AVCaptureVideoDataOutputSampleBufferDelegate).

Built CoreML model using CustomVision to classify images of varying sneaker types. The goal is to train the majority of popular sneakers and determining a sneaker's worth just be quickly scanning. Anticipating newly-released sneaker drops is also an upcoming, desired feature.

App is in store review and waiting to post the app store link.
